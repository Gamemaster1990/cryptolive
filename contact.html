Frequently Asked Questions

Why are some prices delayed?
CryptoLive updates prices every 60 seconds via the CoinGecko API. There may be a slight delay compared to real-time exchange data. For absolute real-time prices, check your exchange directly.
How do I report a bug?
Email us with details about the bug, your browser, and steps to reproduce it.
Can I request a new feature?
Absolutely! We welcome feature suggestions. Send us an email with your idea.
How do I remove my data?
We don't store any personal data. For Google Analytics data, you can use Google's opt-out browser add-on. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
