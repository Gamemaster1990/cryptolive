How to Use CryptoLive

A step-by-step guide to getting the most out of our free crypto price tracker.

💡 No signup required

CryptoLive is completely free and requires no registration, email, or personal information. Just open the page and start tracking.

Getting Started

CryptoLive is designed to be intuitive and accessible to everyone — from crypto beginners to experienced traders. Here's how to use each feature:

Browse Live Prices

When you land on the homepage, you'll see the top 50 cryptocurrencies ranked by market capitalization. Each card displays:

  • Rank — Market cap position (#1, #2, etc.)
  • Coin name & symbol — e.g., Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Current price — Latest price in your chosen currency
  • 24h change — Percentage change over the last 24 hours (green = up, red = down)
  • Sparkline chart — A mini price trend graph for quick visual reference (desktop only)

Search for a Coin

Use the search bar at the top of the page to filter coins by name or ticker symbol. For example, typing "Bit" will show Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, and any other coins containing "bit" in their name or symbol. The results update instantly as you type.

Switch Currencies

Click the currency buttons (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY) to view prices in your preferred fiat currency. The entire price list, market cap, and volume figures will update to reflect the selected currency. Your choice is active for the current session.

Sort Coins

Click the sort options (Rank, Price, 24h %, Name) to reorder the coin list. Click the same option again to reverse the sort order (ascending/descending). This makes it easy to find top gainers, highest-priced coins, or sort alphabetically.

View Coin Details

Click on any coin card to expand it and see additional metrics:

  • Market Cap — Total market capitalization
  • 24h Volume — Trading volume in the last 24 hours
  • 24h High / Low — The highest and lowest prices in the last 24 hours
  • All-Time High — The highest price the coin has ever reached
  • Circulating Supply — Number of coins currently in circulation

Click the coin again to collapse the detail view.

Favorite Coins ★

Click the star icon (☆) on any coin to add it to your favorites. Favorited coins show a filled star (★) and are stored in your browser — they'll persist even after you close and reopen the page. This is useful for keeping an eye on your specific portfolio coins.

Auto-Refresh

Prices automatically refresh every 60 seconds. A green dot in the header indicates active auto-refresh. When you switch to another browser tab, auto-refresh pauses to save bandwidth and resumes when you come back. You can also click the Refresh button at any time to manually update prices.
