Our Mission

Major crypto tracking platforms have become bloated with ads, affiliate promotions, and paywalled features. We built CryptoLive to be the opposite — a clean, fast, no-nonsense dashboard that shows you exactly what you need: prices, changes, and market data. Nothing more, nothing less.

How It Works

CryptoLive fetches real-time data from the CoinGecko API (a free, public cryptocurrency data source). When you visit the site, it loads the top 50 coins by market cap and displays their:

  • Current price (USD)
  • 24-hour price change percentage
  • Market capitalization rank
  • Total market cap and 24h volume (aggregated)

The page automatically refreshes every 60 seconds, so you always see the latest prices without manually reloading.
